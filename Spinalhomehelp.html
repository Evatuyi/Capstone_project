WELCOME TO SPINAL HOME HELP

Spinal Home Help (SHH) volunteers provide one-to-one peer mentoring support to people with spinal cord injuries and their immediate family members.
Support is provided as the client transitions from hospital through rehabilitation to living at home and reengaging with the community.
